About ChipShip

Your favourite store for a PC gaming experience

Who We Are

We are a store focused on customizable PC parts and gaming devices. We provide a wide range of products including keyboards, mice, monitors, CPUs, GPUs, headsets, and other important components. Our goal is to help every customer build a setup that matches their needs, style, and budget.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make building a gaming PC easier and more enjoyable for everyone. We want to give customers access to high-quality parts, useful accessories, and reliable options that can be customized for both gaming and everyday use. We believe every gamer deserves a PC that feels personal and powerful.

Why Choose Us

We offer more than just products — we help customers create a complete gaming experience. With a variety of customizable parts and devices, users can choose exactly what fits their performance needs and design preferences. Whether someone wants a simple setup or a high-end gaming machine, we aim to provide the right tools to make it happen.
